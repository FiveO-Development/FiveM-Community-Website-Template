Cache Clearing Guide

How To Clear Your FiveM Cache

To clear your FiveM cache, first, ensure FiveM is completely closed. Then, open File Explorer and navigate to %localappdata%\FiveM. Delete the "FiveM" folder. Finally, restart FiveM, and it will automatically rebuild the cache.

Detailed Steps:

  • Close FiveM: Make sure FiveM is not running in the background.
  • Navigate to Local App Data: Open File Explorer and type %localappdata% into the address bar and press Enter.
  • Locate the FiveM Folder: You should find the "FiveM" folder within the Local App Data directory.
  • Delete the FiveM Folder: Delete the entire "FiveM" folder.
  • Restart FiveM: Launch FiveM again. It will automatically re-download necessary files and rebuild the cache.

Alternative Method

If you're having trouble clearing the cache, try deleting the "priv" and "server" folders within the FiveM Application Data/Cache folder. This can sometimes resolve issues if deleting the entire folder doesn't work.

When To Clear Cache:

  • After server updates
  • When experiencing texture or model glitches
  • If you encounter connection issues
  • When scripts aren't loading properly

Keybinds Guide

How To Bind A Key in FiveM™

  • Press F8 to open the console
  • Type the following (examples):

Example 1:

bind keyboard F2 escort

Example 2:

bind keyboard F3 givekeys

Example 3:

bind keyboard F4 cuff

Example 4:

bind keyboard F12 impound

Example 5:

bind keyboard F10 refreshskin

Example 6 (shared emotes):

bind keyboard "F9" "nearby handshake"

Example 7 (emotes):

bind keyboard "F3" "e wave"
  • Press ENTER
  • Press F8 to close menu.

NOTES

  • The above listed commands can be copy/pasted into the F8 command box and commands as well as keys can be changed to meet your needs.
  • Any /command can be used.
  • Emotes can also be used but they use a different command line.
  • Any keyboard key can be used but you must know what Windows™ and FiveM™ calls that key.

How to Unbind A Key in FiveM™

  • Press F8
  • Type the following (example key):
unbind keyboard F12
  • Press ENTER
  • Press F8 to close console.
  • Test keybinds.

Jobs Overview

Legal Jobs

Grit City offers numerous legal employment opportunities for citizens looking to make an honest living:

  • Law Enforcement - Maintain law and order as LSPD, BCSO, or State Police
  • Emergency Medical Services - Save lives as an EMT or Paramedic
  • Fire Department - Respond to fires and other emergencies
  • Mechanic - Repair and upgrade vehicles
  • Taxi Driver - Transport citizens around the city
  • Truck Driver - Deliver goods across the map
  • Fisherman - Catch and sell various fish species
  • Miner - Extract valuable minerals from the quarry
  • Real Estate Agent - Help citizens find their dream homes
  • Lawyer - Represent clients in court while working your way thru the ranks of our Department of Justice(whitelist required)
  • Certified Flight Instructor - Take to the skies training all civilian and LEO pilots in Grit City (whitelist required)
  • Airport Ground Crew - Get your start in aviation by guiding and parking aircraft at LSIA
  • Dispatcher - Show off your communication skills as a Certified Dispatcher for all Emergency Services branches(whitelist required)

Criminal Activities

For those with less legitimate aspirations, several illegal activities exist. Remember that criminal actions have consequences and risk factors:

  • Drug Production/Dealing - Manufacture and sell illegal substances
  • Robbery - Target stores, banks, or individuals
  • Car Theft - Steal and sell vehicles
  • Black Market Trading - Deal in illegal goods and contraband
  • Hacking - Bypass electronic security systems

Note: Criminal activities should be roleplayed properly and follow server rules regarding escalation, planning, and NLR (New Life Rule).

Business Ownership

Beyond regular employment, ambitious citizens can establish their own businesses:

  • Restaurants/Cafés - Serve food and beverages
  • Retail Shops - Sell various goods to citizens
  • Nightclubs - Entertainment venues for nightlife
  • Repair Shops - Specialized vehicle services
  • Private Security - Protect clients and properties
  • Entertainment Venues - Start a career in the amusement industry with your own bowling alley or pool hall

Business ownership requires application and approval from roleplay staff and is available only during select time frames(announcements). Contact the roleplay team through the ticket system inside Discord for more information about starting your own business.

Economy System

Currency Overview

Grit City uses a carefully balanced economy system designed to provide realistic progression and meaningful financial choices:

  • The standard currency is USD ($)
  • New citizens start with $5,000 cash / $30,000 in the bank to begin their life in Grit City
  • Bank accounts are available at all Maze Bank locations
  • ATMs are located throughout the city for convenient access
  • Money can be stored as cash (in inventory) or in bank accounts
  • Cash transfers between players are possible through the inventory menu
  • Bank transfers require the recipient's account details

Income Sources

There are multiple ways to earn money in Grit City:

  • Legal Jobs - Steady income with minimal risk
  • Business Ownership - Higher potential earnings with investment required
  • Investments - Stock market trading and property ownership
  • Criminal Activities - Higher risk but potentially higher rewards
  • Item Crafting - Create and sell specialized items
  • Saving/Interest - Hold your valuable income in a savings account and benefit from interest earnings
  • Trading - Buy low, sell high on various commodities

Major Expenses

Managing finances requires balancing several ongoing expenses:

  • Property Costs - Apartment rent or house mortgages
  • Vehicle Expenses - Purchase, customization, insurance, and repairs
  • Taxes - Income tax on legal earnings (automatically deducted)
  • Food and Basic Needs - Regular consumption requirements
  • Medical Care - Hospital bills after injuries
  • Legal Fees - Court costs, fines, and lawyer fees
  • Business Overhead - Staff salaries, supplies, and utilities

Money Laundering

Criminal earnings often come as "dirty money" which must be laundered before it can be deposited in banks or used for major purchases:

  • Dirty money has a distinct inventory item appearance
  • Various laundering methods exist (discovered through roleplay)
  • Laundering typically incurs a percentage fee
  • Carrying large amounts of dirty money increases risk if caught by law enforcement

Note: Money laundering mechanics are intentionally not fully documented - discover them through roleplay and character connections.
