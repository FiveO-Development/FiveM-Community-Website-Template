Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Grit City Roleplay collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of our website and FiveM server.

Last Updated: May 14, 2025

Important Notice

By using Grit City Roleplay's website, FiveM server, Discord, or any associated services, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

1. Introduction

Grit City Roleplay ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy applies to the Grit City Roleplay website, FiveM server, Discord server, and all related services (collectively, the "Services") and explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

We take your privacy seriously and will only use your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Services, including:

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect information such as your username, email address, Discord ID, and password.
  • Profile Information: Information you provide in your user profile, such as profile pictures, biography, or social media links.
  • Communications: Information you provide when you contact us for support, report issues, or communicate with our staff or other users.
  • Payment Information: When you make purchases through our VIP Store, payment information is collected by our third-party payment processor (Tebex). We do not directly collect or store payment details such as credit card numbers.
  • Application Information: Information you provide when applying to join our server, including answers to application questions, character backstories, or previous roleplay experience.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Technical Information: IP address, device information, browser type and version, operating system, and other technical information about your device and internet connection.
  • Gameplay Data: In-game activities, character interactions, chat logs, gameplay statistics, playtime, and other data related to your use of our FiveM server.
  • Server Logs: Connection and disconnection times, server errors, and other technical logs that help us maintain and improve our Services.
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our website and Services, including pages visited, links clicked, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Discord Integration: When you connect your Discord account, we receive information such as your Discord username, ID, and avatar.
  • FiveM Identity: Information associated with your FiveM account, including your FiveM ID and license.
  • Social Media: If you interact with our social media pages or share content from our Services, we may receive information from those social media platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide and Maintain our Services: Create and manage user accounts, deliver in-game features and functionality, and ensure the proper functioning of our Services.
  • Improve and Develop our Services: Analyze usage patterns, identify areas for improvement, develop new features, and enhance the user experience.
  • Ensure Safety and Security: Monitor and enforce compliance with our Terms of Service and server rules, prevent cheating and abuse, and protect the integrity of our Services.
  • Personalize User Experience: Tailor content and features to individual users, remember user preferences, and provide a consistent experience across our Services.
  • Communication: Send administrative notices, updates, and support messages; respond to inquiries; and provide important information about our Services.
  • Process Transactions: Complete purchases, deliver virtual goods, and maintain records of transactions.
  • Community Management: Facilitate interaction between users, moderate content, and maintain a positive community environment.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, and protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users or the public.

4.1 With Your Consent

We may share your information when you give us explicit consent to do so.

We may share information with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as:

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency). We may also disclose your information to:

If Grit City Roleplay is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your information.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information about your interactions with our website.

5.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the operation of our website and Services. These enable core functionality such as security, account management, and remembering user preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Functionality Cookies: Enable us to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages they visit most often.

5.2 Your Cookie Choices

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, if you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of the Services may be inaccessible or not function properly.

5.3 Do Not Track Signals

We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals or similar mechanisms transmitted by web browsers.

When you make purchases through our VIP Store, your payment information is collected and processed by Tebex, our third-party payment processor. Their use of your information is governed by their privacy policy. We encourage you to review Tebex's privacy policy before making a purchase.

7. Discord Integration

Our Services integrate with Discord for community management, authentication, and communication purposes.

7.1 Discord OAuth

When you connect your Discord account to our Services, we use the Discord OAuth2 protocol to authenticate your identity and access certain information from your Discord account, such as your username, Discord ID, and avatar. This integration helps us verify your identity and streamline the login process.

7.2 Discord Bot

We may operate a Discord bot that collects and processes information within our Discord server. The bot may collect information such as:

  • Messages sent in specific channels
  • Commands issued to the bot
  • Voice channel activity
  • Role and permission changes

This information is used to provide community features, enforce server rules, and enhance the user experience within our Discord community.

7.3 Discord Data Sharing

Information may be shared between our Services and our Discord server to:

  • Sync roles and permissions
  • Display in-game information in Discord
  • Verify account ownership
  • Facilitate communication between platforms

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Our security measures include:

  • Using secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS) for our website
  • Storing passwords in a hashed and salted format
  • Implementing access controls to limit who can access user data
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Staff training on data protection practices

While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials and should notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account or other security breaches.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

9.1 Account Information

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Services. If you delete your account or request that we delete your information, we will delete your account information within 30 days, except for information that we are required to retain for legitimate business or legal purposes.

9.2 Server Logs and Gameplay Data

Server logs and gameplay data may be retained for varying periods:

  • Technical logs for server performance and security: 30-90 days
  • Chat logs and player interactions: Up to 180 days
  • Character progression and in-game assets: For the duration of the character's existence

9.3 Anonymized Data

We may retain anonymized, aggregated data indefinitely for analytical purposes and to improve our Services.
